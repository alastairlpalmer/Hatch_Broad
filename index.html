Why Hatch

The smarter workplace
food solution.

Everything your building needs. Nothing you need to manage.

01

Replace your canteen costs overnight

Traditional workplace catering means kitchens, chefs, and six-figure staffing bills. Hatch gives your people better food for a fraction of the cost, with nothing to build and no one to hire.

02

Takes up less space than a desk

One plug socket and under 1 m² of floor space. That servery area you're losing rent on? You get it back. For a typical London office, that's £20k–£40k a year in recovered real estate.

03

One monthly fee. That's it.

No procurement headaches, no supplier juggling, no surprise invoices. A single subscription covers the fridge, the food, restocking, maintenance and the tech — all taken care of.

04

The perk people actually use

Ping-pong tables gather dust. Good food brings people in. Over 40% of hybrid workers say on-site food is the single biggest reason to come to the office. Hatch makes that easy to deliver.

05

See exactly what your people want

Every purchase is tracked. You get a monthly report showing what sells, when demand peaks, and how your team's preferences change week to week. We use that data to keep the menu sharp.

06

Food safety you never have to think about

Every fridge is temperature controlled 24/7 through our remote systems and the digital display surfaces any allergens. We carry full liability cover so your risk exposure for workplace snacks is zero.
